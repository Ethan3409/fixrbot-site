What Is FixrBot?

FixrBot is your AI assistant for Flutter debugging. Whether you're stuck on mysterious build errors or strange behavior in your UI, FixrBot gives you a senior developer's insight—without the hourly rates.

Choose Your Fixing Style

FixrBot offers two flat-rate services tailored for speed and simplicity:

  • Standard Fix – Ideal for typical debugging needs with delivery in 48 hours.
  • Priority Fix – Fast-track service for urgent fixes delivered in under 24 hours.

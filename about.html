Our Mission

FixrBot aims to eliminate tedious debugging so you can ship better Flutter apps faster. We blend AI diagnostics with best‑practice patches to get your code production‑ready in hours, not days.

The Story

Created by Ethan Fischer, FixrBot was born from real‑world pain: spending nights squashing regressions instead of building features. Today, FixrBot serves indie devs and small teams who need an instant senior developer on call 24 / 7.
